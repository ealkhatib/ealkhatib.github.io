About

My name is Ehab Al Khatib. I am currently working toward a PH.D. degree at SMU under the supervision of Professor Yildirim Hurmuzlu. I have had many opportunities not only to tackle cutting-edge problems from multidisciplinary perspectives, but also to sharpen different skills and programming languages. My research work has been interdisciplinary, ranging from system modeling to linear/nonlinear controls of electromechanical, embedded and robotic systems designed on a multitude of sizes spanning the micro to macro scales.
